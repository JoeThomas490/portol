## Description

Cafe on the Edge of the Galaxy was a Global Game Jam 2018 project I took on with fellow students from Sheffield Hallam University.

> You are a human worker in an alien cafe; you must make sandwiches for hungry customers, and pay close attention to their foreign language! You don't want to upset them. See how much money you can make before your shift ends.

This was a great project to learn new skills, working in a team of new developers and trying to create something genuinely fun within a limited time.
